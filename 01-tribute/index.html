Clifford Brown

Jazz trumpet player influential in the hard-bop style

Photo of Clifford Brown (1930-1956) holding his trumpet.

Timeline of Clifford Brown's life:

  • 1930 - Born in Wilmington, DE into a musical family
  • 1943 - Received his first trumpet, and starts taking private lessons from his father at age 13
  • 1950 - Played music in college at Maryland State and professionally in Philidelphia, working with musicians such as Fats Navarro and Dizzy Gillespie
  • 1953 - Worked with the drummer Max Roach, leading to the best-known recordings of both musicians
  • 1956 - Died tragically in a car wreck on a rainy night in Bedford, PA while driving to a gig in Chicago
